BRNO php

Our goal is to create a community of php programmers in Brno. In doing so we are organizing events where developers can listen to talks, chat, have a good beer and most importantly have a good time together.

Meetups

We try to organize meetup every 2 months. Usually these meetups are sponsored by different sponsors who provide free food for all attendees
