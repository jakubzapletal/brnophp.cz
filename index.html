BRNO php April meetup

We've decided to switch our website and public communication of BRNO php group to english. Also our main RSPV site is now meetup.com. This decision doesn't make any change to our google+ community. Our plan there is to get more members and create a nice place to have discussion about php and web development in general.

Free entry, registration needed!

Registrations are limited to 50 attendees!

Talks

We will have 4 talks, 25 minutes for each.

  • 18:20 When become developer a part of management (Marek Šudák)
  • 19:10 WebSockets - how to do real-time applications in PHP (Ondřej Mirtes)
  • 20:00 ORM Designer: Speed up your ORM development (František Tröster)
  • 20:50 How to parallelize in PHP and not go crazy (Martin Strouhal)

Bonuses

BRNO php

Our goal is to create a community of php programmers in Brno. In doing so we are organizing events where developers can listen to talks, chat, have a good beer and most importantly have a good time together.

Meetups

We try to organize meetup every 2 months. Usually these meetups are sponsored by different sponsors who provide free food for all attendees
